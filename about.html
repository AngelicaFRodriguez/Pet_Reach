Pet Reach was started to help boost exposure for available cats and dogs in the San Antonio area. There are so many wonderful animals in our shelters and rescues waiting for homes. Adopting these animals helps our city's overpopulation issue and saves their lives.
My name is Angelica and I was inspired to create Pet Reach because all of my pets were adopted from ACS or SAFCC and each of them are great animals I couldn't live without.
